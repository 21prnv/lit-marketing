Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

Lit Marketing ("we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and the choices you have. By using our website or services, you agree to the practices described here.

1. Information We Collect

  • Information you give us: name, email address, brand name, social handles, and any details you submit through our contact form or via email.
  • Automatically collected data: IP address, browser type, device information, pages visited, and referring URLs, gathered through cookies and similar technologies.
  • Client campaign data: for clients, we may process analytics, ad performance, and audience data from connected social and advertising platforms.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To respond to inquiries and provide quotes or proposals.
  • To deliver, manage, and improve our marketing services.
  • To send updates, newsletters, or promotional messages (you can opt out anytime).
  • To analyze website performance and improve user experience.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and analytics tools (such as Google Analytics and advertising platform pixels) to understand how visitors use our site and to measure campaign performance. You can disable cookies through your browser settings, though some features may not work as intended.

4. Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers who help us operate (e.g., hosting, email, analytics, advertising platforms).
  • Legal authorities when required by law or to protect our rights.
  • Business transfers in connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

5. Data Retention

We keep personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described in this policy, or as required by law. When no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it.

6. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the use of your personal data, and to opt out of marketing communications. To exercise these rights, email us at hello@litmarketing.co.

7. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

8. Third-Party Links

Our site and content may link to third-party platforms (e.g., TikTok, Instagram, YouTube). We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those services. Please review their policies separately.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to individuals under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes take effect when posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.

11. Contact Us

Questions about this policy? Reach out at hello@litmarketing.co.
